Quick Overview

We provide a photo/logo laser engraving service for your own Dog Tag, ID Tag or Military Tag. Your picture or logo can be engraved in full colour, black & white or etched onto the pendant. To customize it even more you can have a message engraved on the reverse. 


Our requirements to approve job:



  • photo area flat & smooth

  • chain removed beforehand

  • solid Gold or Silver (or other precious metals)

  • max. dimensions 50x50mm

Details

Have your ID Tag, Dog Tag or Military Tag laser engraved with your desired picture. You can personalize your order with multiple photos engraved and much more. Perfect if you are preparing a very personal gift or just creating a keepsake for yourself.

Requirements for job:

  • flat & smooth surface
  • no bale attached / bale flat with surface
  • chain removed beforehand
  • solid Gold or Silver (or other precious metals)
  • max. dimensions 50x50mm
